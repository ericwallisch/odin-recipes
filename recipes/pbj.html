Peanut Butter and Jelly

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Half a jar of peanut butter
  • Jelly
  1. Get two slices of bread and put those suckers on a plate.
  2. Slather one in peanut butter and slather the other in jelly.
  3. Close that bad boy up and enjoy.

Pro tip: don't forget to give the dogs the knife to lick.
