Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 8 slices of cheese
  • Butter
  1. Get two slices of bread and put those suckers on a plate.
  2. Slather one side of each piece in butter
  3. Put all the cheese between the two pieces of bread with the butter facing out.
  4. Grill that bad boy on both sides.
  5. Enjoy.

Pro tip: don't forget to give the dogs the knife to lick.
